Weather Intelligence

Inventory Management

Financial Dashboard

Revenue tracking, expense monitoring, instructor payroll, and booking income — all in real time with exportable reports.

Cross Communication

Centralized messaging between school, instructors, and students. No more WhatsApp chaos — one thread per booking.

Waivers & Forms

Multi-Currency

Display prices in local currency with USD/EUR toggle. Accept payments worldwide — never store converted amounts.

Excursion Planner

Plan and sell guided excursions — downwinders, island trips, spot tours. Participant tracking, gear assignment, and deposits.

Repair Tickets

Log gear damage, assign repair status, track turnaround time. Know exactly what's flight-ready and what's grounded.

Smart Notifications

Booking confirmations, weather alerts, low inventory warnings, and daily summary emails — all automated.

Analytics & Reports

Booking trends, revenue by service, instructor performance, student retention — data-driven decisions made simple.

School Settings

Booking rules, cancellation policies, deposit percentages, timezone, language, and notification preferences — fully customizable.

Public School Profile

Your school's public listing on The Kite Alliance — discoverable by riders worldwide. Reviews, services, location, and direct booking.
